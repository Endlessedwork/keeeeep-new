# Privacy Policy for Keeeeep

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Keeeeep ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and password
- **Bookmark Data**: URLs, titles, descriptions, tags, and categories you save
- **User-Generated Content**: Notes and custom descriptions you add to bookmarks

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Website Metadata**: When you save a bookmark, we fetch publicly available information (title, description, images) from the URL
- **AI-Generated Summaries**: We use OpenAI's API to generate Thai language summaries of your bookmarks

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Keeeeep service
- Generate AI summaries of bookmarked websites
- Organize and categorize your bookmarks
- Improve user experience

## Data Storage

**Local Storage**: All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do NOT store your data on our servers.

**Cloud Services**: We use OpenAI's API to generate summaries. Website URLs and metadata are sent to OpenAI for processing. Please review [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy).

## Data Security

- Passwords are stored locally on your device
- We do not transmit your personal data to third parties except for AI processing
- Your bookmarks remain private and local to your device

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **OpenAI GPT-4**: For AI-powered content summarization
- Website metadata is fetched directly from the URLs you provide

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data by uninstalling the app
- Export your data (through app features)

## Children's Privacy

Keeeeep is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: endlessedwork@gmail.com
- Developer: Endlessedwork

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Uninstall the Keeeeep app from your device
2. All locally stored data will be permanently removed
